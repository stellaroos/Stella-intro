Introducing Stella Roos

Stella Roos is a Finnish journalist focused on the built environment. She is the design correspondent for Monocle and a contributor to Konfekt Magazine, AD Germany and Observer Magazine, among others. She holds a bachelors degree with honours in European Social and Political Studies from University College London.
